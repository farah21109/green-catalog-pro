

# Digital Catalogue Website — Power Solutions Factory

A professional, modern digital catalogue website for a power equipment & energy solutions manufacturer. New design with a fresh color theme (deep navy blue & amber/gold accents), improved layout, and better UX — fully responsive for desktop and mobile.

## Pages & Sections (Single-Page with Smooth Scroll Navigation)

### 1. Top Bar & Navigation
- Contact info bar (phone, email, GST number, response rate badge)
- Sticky navbar with logo, company name & tagline
- Menu links: Home, About Us, Products, Dealer Certificates, Services, Contact Us
- "Get Best Price Now" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu with all links properly connected via anchor scrolling

### 2. Hero Section (Image Carousel)
- Auto-sliding carousel with 3 slides: Solar Panels, Industrial Inverters, High-Performance Batteries
- Each slide: background image, headline, description, two CTA buttons
- Navigation dots and arrows
- Stats bar below hero: 15+ Years Experience, 5000+ Customers, 500+ Dealers, 50+ Products

### 3. About Us Section
- Company description as a trusted Indian manufacturer
- Key highlights: ISO Certified, 500+ Dealers, 15+ Years Experience, BIS Approved
- Company details table: Nature of Business, Employees, Legal Status, Annual Turnover
- Mini stats cards

### 4. Products Section (Digital Catalogue — No Prices)
- 6 product categories displayed as cards:
  - Inverters (4 products)
  - Batteries (4 products)
  - UPS Systems (4 products)
  - Solar Panels (4 products)
  - Solar Inverters (4 products)
  - Lithium Batteries (4 products)
- Each category card: image, title, description, product list with key specs/features
- "Get Best Price Now" button on each (links to contact form)
- **No prices displayed** — dealers inquire via contact form

### 5. Dealer Certificates & Accreditations Section
- 6 certificate/award cards with images, year, title, and description
- "Become a Dealer" CTA banner

### 6. Services Section
- 6 service cards with icons: Installation, Annual Maintenance, 24/7 Support, Warranty Services, Site Survey, Training Programs
- Each with description and "Learn More" link to contact

### 7. Contact Section
- Contact form: Name, Email, Phone, Product Interest dropdown, Requirements textarea
- "Get Best Price Now" submit button
- Contact info sidebar: Address, Phone, Email, Business Hours

### 8. Footer
- Company info, quick links, and copyright

## Design & UX Improvements
- **Color theme**: Deep navy & amber/gold instead of green — more premium feel
- **Typography**: Clean, modern font hierarchy
- **Layout**: Card-based product grid instead of stacked sections for easier browsing
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in effects on scroll
- **Mobile**: Fully responsive with collapsible menu and stacked layouts
- **All menu links** properly connected via smooth scroll to anchored sections
